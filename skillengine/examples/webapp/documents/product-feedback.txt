PRODUCT FEEDBACK SUMMARY — Q1 2026
Source: Customer surveys (n=214), in-app NPS responses, support ticket themes
Compiled by: Product Team

---

NET PROMOTER SCORE: 47 (up from 39 in Q4 2025)

---

TOP POSITIVE THEMES

1. Speed of setup (mentioned by 61% of promoters)
   "We were running our first automated report in under an hour. I've never seen that before."
   "The Salesforce connector just worked. No back and forth with IT."

2. Report quality (mentioned by 49% of promoters)
   "Our CMO actually said the weekly dashboard looks better than what our previous agency produced."
   "The templates save us hours every week."

3. Customer support (mentioned by 44% of promoters)
   "Every ticket I've opened has been resolved same day. That's rare."
   "The onboarding specialist stayed on the call until everything was working. Really above and beyond."

---

TOP NEGATIVE THEMES

1. Missing CRM integrations (mentioned by 38% of detractors)
   "We're on Zoho and there's no native connector. We had to use Zapier which added latency."
   "HubSpot free tier is not supported — we have to upgrade to use the integration which feels like a bait and switch."

2. Mobile experience (mentioned by 31% of detractors)
   "The dashboard looks terrible on my phone. I check it on my commute and it's basically unusable."
   "Need a proper mobile app or at least a responsive layout."

3. Export limitations (mentioned by 27% of detractors)
   "PDF exports don't support custom fonts. Our brand guide requires Helvetica Neue."
   "Can't export raw data to CSV from the dashboard — have to go through the API which defeats the purpose."

4. Pricing transparency (mentioned by 19% of detractors)
   "Didn't realize data connectors beyond the first two were add-on costs. Should be clearer at signup."

---

FEATURE REQUESTS (ranked by volume)

1. Mobile app / responsive design — 89 requests
2. Additional CRM connectors (Zoho, Pipedrive, Copper) — 76 requests
3. Custom font support in PDF exports — 54 requests
4. Slack / Teams alert integration — 48 requests
5. Multi-user permission roles — 41 requests
6. Historical data import beyond 24 months — 33 requests

---

CHURN RISK FLAGS

- 14 accounts flagged for low engagement (< 2 logins in 30 days)
- 6 accounts cited competitor evaluation as reason for reduced usage
- Primary competitor mentioned: Tableau + Fivetran combo ("more flexible, we already have licenses")
