# Competitive Analysis: Marketing Analytics Platforms
**Date:** March 2026
**Scope:** Direct and adjacent competitors in the B2B marketing analytics space

---

## Executive Summary

Our platform competes primarily against legacy MAPs with bolt-on analytics (Marketo, HubSpot), BI generalists positioned for marketing (Tableau, Looker), and emerging AI-native tools (Charthop, Factors.ai). Our strongest differentiation is speed-to-value and opinionated marketing-specific templates.

---

## Competitor Profiles

### 1. HubSpot Marketing Analytics
**Pricing:** $800–$3,200/mo (Marketing Hub Professional/Enterprise)
**Target:** SMB to mid-market
**Strengths:**
- All-in-one platform reduces integration friction
- Intuitive UI with low learning curve
- Strong community and documentation

**Weaknesses:**
- Analytics limited to HubSpot-native data unless on expensive Enterprise tier
- Cannot connect to external data sources without Operations Hub add-on
- Reporting customization is shallow — no custom calculated fields

**Our advantage:** Multi-source data unification at a lower price point; deeper custom reporting.

---

### 2. Marketo Engage (Adobe)
**Pricing:** $895–$3,175+/mo
**Target:** Mid-market to enterprise
**Strengths:**
- Deep CRM integration (especially Salesforce)
- Sophisticated lead scoring and attribution models
- Enterprise-grade compliance and security

**Weaknesses:**
- Extremely complex to implement (avg. 6–12 week onboarding)
- UI has not meaningfully modernized since 2019 acquisition
- High total cost of ownership including required implementation partners

**Our advantage:** 10x faster onboarding; modern UI; no implementation partner dependency.

---

### 3. Tableau (Salesforce)
**Pricing:** $75/user/mo (Explorer) to $115/user/mo (Creator)
**Target:** Enterprise, data team-forward
**Strengths:**
- Best-in-class visualization and custom dashboard capability
- Connects to virtually any data source
- Large talent pool of Tableau-trained analysts

**Weaknesses:**
- Requires dedicated analyst to build and maintain dashboards
- Marketing teams without data resources cannot self-serve
- No built-in marketing attribution models — must be built from scratch

**Our advantage:** Marketing-specific templates; no analyst required; built-in attribution out of the box.

---

### 4. Factors.ai
**Pricing:** $499–$1,499/mo
**Target:** Growth-stage B2B SaaS
**Strengths:**
- Strong account-level intent signal integration
- Modern UI and fast product iteration
- Good Slack integration for real-time alerts

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited historical data (< 18 months)
- Attribution model is opinionated and not customizable
- No PDF or scheduled report export

**Our advantage:** Longer data history; customizable attribution; scheduled delivery to executives.
